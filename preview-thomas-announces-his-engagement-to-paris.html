In the latest The Bold and the Beautiful preview for June 24 – 28, how does Poppy know Tom? Read on and watch the preview below.

Bill has been on cloud nine playing house with his former flame Poppy and his supposed daughter Luna. Coming up Poppy answers the door to Bill’s house, well now her and Luna’s house too, and finds Tom standing there with a pizza from Il Giardano! She is shunned and asks, “What are you doing here? Could Tom actually be Luna’s father?
