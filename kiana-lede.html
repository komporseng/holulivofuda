Career and Professional Life

In 2011, Lede’s mother found Kidz Bop, a website that allows kids to upload and share videos of themselves performing, and she submitted a video of Lede playing an original piano song that was selected as a winner.

Then, in 2011, Lede won Kidz Bop’s “2011 Kidz Star USA” talent competition, gaining a recording deal with RCA Records.

She also appeared in the music video and ad for Kidz Bop 21. “Do the Mermaid,” her first record song included in the animated film Barbie in A Mermaid Tale 2 (2012).

She released her debut song, Hey Chica, on June 19, 2012, an upbeat melody dedicated to female empowerment and encouraging young women to feel attractive in their flesh.

The song was an instant smash, and she rose to fame. Miss Moxie’s Holiday 2012 edition featured her as the cover girl.

On NBC’s “The Today Show” and at the “86th Annual Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade,” she sang the song.

Her first EP, “Soulfood Sessions,” was released in December 2015 under the Orange Factory Music label.  “Christmas by Lede,” her sophomore EP was released on December 12, 2016. ”

Home for Christmas,” a non-album single, was also released the same year. “I Choose You,” “One of Them Days,” and “Fairplay” were among her hits released the following year.

Other EP and albums

Her third EP, “Selfless,” was released on July 12, 2018, and featured the tracks EX and a Fairplay remix with ASAP Ferg.

Kiana’s original composition “Fairplay” from the album “Selfless” was shot in New Orleans, as was the video for it.

The official video for “Ex” had over 47 million views on her YouTube channel, and it reached number 6 on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles chart, number 9 on the Hot R&B Songs Chart, and number 44 on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs Chart. As of June 2020, the RIAA has awarded its platinum certification.

The singles “Can I” and “Bouncin” were among them. The single “Bouncin,” which features rapper Offset and charts in the top 30 on Billboard’s rhythmic radio chart, was released in February.

On April 3, 2020, she launched her debut album, “Kiki,” which features tracks like “Mad At Me,” “Forfeit,” “Chocolate,” “Only Fan,” and others. It debuted at number 30 on the Billboard 200 and received generally positive reviews.

Acting career

Kiana has a successful acting career in addition to her music career. In 2006, she acted in the Vimeo TV show “Temporary Dreams” as herself.

Moreover, in the same year, she made her film debut in the film “Deadlands: The Rising,” as Evacuee/Zombie.

In 2015, she appeared as Riley in two episodes of the American teen drama online series “Guidance,” “The Pictures” and “The Secrets.”

Moreover, in 2015 and 2016, she also played numerous characters in the TV series “Beach.” In addition, in 2016, she made her breakthrough as Zoe Vaughn in season 2 of MTV’s horror series “Scream.”

In 2018, she appeared in the Netflix series “All About the Washingtons” as Veronica Washington.

She also voiced Greenwood in the computer-animated science fiction action film “NextGen” in the same year. The video inspires by Wang Nima’s online Chinese comic 7723.
