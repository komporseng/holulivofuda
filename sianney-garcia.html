Sianney Garcia Early Life:

Sianney Garcia was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, in the United States and raised partly in Philadelphia and the suburbs of The City Of Brotherly Love.

Sianney Garcia Education:

Sianney Garcia attended st veronica catholic School in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, with her twin sister. Both the twins were homeschooled after graduating to focus on their music careers.

Sianney Garcia Career:

Sianney loved gymnastics and hoped that she would participate in the junior nationals one day. Still, as she grew up, she became more attracted to music and rap.

Sianney, along with her twin sister, Angelise, performed at their brother’s engagement. To their amazement, the guests present loved their performance so much that they decided to form a musical group together. They named it SiAngie twins. At the encouragement of their older brother, Danny, the two created a YouTube channel on December 5, 2011. The account was created under the name Sianneyangelise. the channel has garnered 25,774,663 views until now.

The first-ever video they posted on their YouTube channel is drake – shot for me covering 10 yr old Angelise Garcia. and has garnered a total of 135k views to date. The two covered several well-known songs by drake and Emeli Sande during their early times. Sianney and Angelise came to prominence with their debut single, After School, released in July 2013 and re-released in 2014. Due to a high interest in what they could do, in 2014, she and her sister also went on to release a track named Butterflies and Can’t-Wait for Christmas and their debut EP, My All 2014. in 2015 the twins diversified the content they posted on their YouTube channel.

They posted challenges such as egg roulette, smoothie, and twin telepathy and connected with their fans through storytime and Q&A videos. Angie Twins’ busiest year came in 2018 and saw the release of their 2nd EP, Innocent Savages, and a track such as Splash ft. Dream Doll. Sianney and her sister also released songs such as Crazy Love, Forever, and Watch Me Go featuring Mikey Woodz in 2019.
