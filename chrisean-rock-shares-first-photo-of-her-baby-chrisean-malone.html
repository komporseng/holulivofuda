CHRISEAN ROCK SHARES FIRST PHOTO OF HER BABY, CHRISEAN MALONE

June 26, 2024
blog

Chrisean Rock has shared the first photo of her baby boy, Chrisean Malone.

“1day old ….Heavensent you healthy and so handsome beyond grateful. My baby boy so blessed. Thank you Jesus,” she captioned the snap on Instagram.

Rock welcomed her son with rapper BlueFace via Instagram Live on September 3rd. She later took to social media to share that she had named the child after herself, calling him Chrisean Malone.

In addition, the reality star revealed that she had chosen not to have BlueFace present in the delivery room. 

Upon learning about the name chosen for their son, Blueface promptly took to social media to express his discontent.

He remarked, “I actually feel bad for my son at this point he’s a junior to his mom he don’t gotta be named after me but at least put some thought into it that was some quick last minute….what do you want to name him uhm just write down [Chrisean] Malone smh. Should have just named the baby ‘clout’ would have been more meaningful.”

In response to Blueface’s comments, Chrisean didn’t hold back on Twitter, sharing her desire to move forward in a positive manner.

“Stop the bitter [stuff] we kno you dropping another song soon I get it … let me heal fr stop trynna make something about another woman knowing u will Leave her anyday for me. Yo son will be waiting for his Dadddy to grow tf up….”

Chrisean emphasized that the ongoing feud was primarily between her and BlueFace, urging him to stop using their issues for promotional purposes. She also clarified her intention to separate herself from the drama.

“Johnathan let’s go head up I don’t care what u got going on the beef is between you and I you include the other [stuff] for promotional reasons. I left with nothing on purpose so u won’t even be able to say it had something to do with MY MONEY. just go heal n love yo family properly.”

She concluded her heartfelt message by expressing her hope for healing and for the blessings to surround them all. She made it clear that this would be her last public mention of their issues.

“Time will heal everything but this is my last tweet /post /comment mentioning you ever again. You have my number; the social media stuff is getting ridiculous. Just heal n let the blessings just invade everything n everyone. Me and chriseanjr will always love you unconditionally.”

In turn, BlueFace responded to Chrisean’s tweets, expressing his desire to make amends for the sake of their child.

 “I’m just tryna make it right at this point cuz there is a baby involved an it’s deeper then rap an went further then it should have but y’all won’t let me…”
