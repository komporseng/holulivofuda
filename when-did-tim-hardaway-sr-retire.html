When did Tim Hardaway Sr. retire?

July 01, 2024
blog

Tim Hardaway Sr. retired from professional basketball on October 26, 2003. After a successful career spanning over 13 seasons, he decided to hang up his jersey and move on to the next chapter of his life. Hardaway was known for his exceptional skills as a point guard and his trademark crossover dribble. He played for various teams throughout his career, including the Golden State Warriors, Miami Heat, Dallas Mavericks, Denver Nuggets, and Indiana Pacers. Despite facing injuries towards the end of his career, Hardaway will always be remembered as one of the greatest point guards of his generation.
