Later, she and other female contestants were given the power to decide which male singles they don’t want to pursue further, and vice versa. This meant that the remaining singles were to continue their pursuit of love, navigating love triangles, drama, and broken hearts along the way.

Overall, Ready to Love promised an exciting and emotional rollercoaster ride as the singles embarked on their quest for lasting love. With its focus on genuine connections, the show offered a refreshing twist on the traditional dating reality format.
